F.A.Q

We have listed some mostly asked questions by customers with answers.

  • Where can I find box number (STB Card Number) ?

    1. You can find your box number behind the box on white sticker as Card Number: (Starting with 603063700......./ 776033700.......)

    2. You can also find your box number on tv > MENU > SETTING > INFORMATION >CARD NO:

  • I have 2 or more box is it possible to make first box HD (High Definition) and second box SD (Standard Definition) ?

    The answer is No. In a same customer's account only one package can be assigned.

  • How can I pay Max Digital TV bill ?

    1. You can pay your Max Digital TV bill through Esewa and Khalti app. Tutorials
    2. You can request your respective area's collector for topup. Find your collector
    3. You can visit bracnch office during office time. Find branch office near your

  • What is Residential and Commercal Customers ?

    1. Residential customers are those who can have less or equal to 4 boxes.
    2. Commercial customers are those who must have more or equal to 5 boxes. For this subscription PAN number must me needed.

  • How to do factory default or Search ?

    Step 1. Go to Menu
    Step 2. Go to Setting
    Step 3. Go to Factory Defaulty and press OK
    Step 4. Enter passwort: 0000 and OK
    Caution: Do not exit channels searching !

  • What does E14 mean ?

    1. E14 means "Your subscription has been expired" Pay Now
    2. If your subscription has not been expired yet you need to contact for help Contact Now

  • Green light not blinking ?

    Mostly there may be an issue in your connection or due to some issues signal in your area may be down. Need to contact for support.

  • What should we do to use another operator box in Pokhara ?

    Step 1. You need to inform previous cable operator to disconnect your box.
    Step 2. You need to resgister your details in Pokhara.
